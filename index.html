Data Narrative

The Cost of Living in Malaysia

An analytical study on price evolution and household economic pressure across the Federation.

Headline inflation looks moderate, but CPI and household income data reveal sharper pressure in specific states.

Executive Summary

Regional price pressure is uneven.

  • Structural Shifts Post-2020 costs reset higher.
  • Regional Divergence Growth gaps widened after 2020.
  • Burden Concentration Household buffers are uneven.
  • Policy Implication National CPI masks local pressure.
